Chunky Sweater

Pattern is worked in the smallest size, larger sizes will be listed in order within the ( ) Yarn: weight 5, used 1,300 yards of yarn for size small (4skeins) Hook size: 6.5 MM

  • SLEEVES
    Ribbing: Ch 13. 1st row: 1 sc in 2nd ch from hook. 1 sc in each ch to end of chain. Turn. 12 sc.
  • 2nd row: Ch 1. Working in back loops only, 1 sc in each sc to end of row. Turn. Rep last row for total 27 (29medium,31large,33extra-large) ending on a RS row. Do not turn or fasten off . Work across long edge of Ribbing as follows:
  • Next row: (RS). Ch 1. Work 33 (37-39-41) sc evenly across. Turn.
  • Next row: Ch 3 (counts as dc). *1 sc in next sc. 1 dc in next sc. Rep from * to end of row. Turn.
  • Next row: Ch 3 (counts as dc). 1 sc in rst dc (inc made). *1 dc in next sc. 1 sc in next dc. Rep from * to end of row, working (1 sc. 1 dc) in top of ch 3 (inc made). Turn.
  • Next row: Ch 1. 1 sc in first dc. *1 dc in next sc. 1 sc in next dc. Rep from * to end of row, working last sc in top of ch 3. Turn. Next row: Ch 1. (1 sc. 1 dc) in first sc (inc made). *1 sc in next dc. 1 dc in next sc. Rep from * to end of row, working (1 dc. 1 sc) in last sc (inc made). Turn.
  • Next row: Ch 3 (counts as dc). *1 sc in next dc. 1 dc in next sc. Rep from * to end of row. Turn. Keeping count of Texture Pat, inc 1 st each end of next row and following RS rows 2 (3-4-5) times more, taking inc sts into pat. Work 4 rows no increase.
  • Make elbow ridges: 1st row: (RS). Ch 2. 2 hdc in rst st. 1 hdc in each st to last st. 2 hdc in last st. Turn.
  • 2nd row: Ch 2. *Work 1 hdc in horizontal bar created between sts of previous row. repeat until end of row. turn crochet 8 rows no increase.
  • 11th row, increase in 1st and last chain of row
  • 12th-13th row no increase. cont. with original sc/dc pattern until you reach the desired length for sleeves.
  • BACK
    Ribbing: Ch 13. 1st row: 1 sc in 2nd ch from hook. 1 sc in each ch to end of chain. Turn. 12 sc.
  • 2nd row: Ch 1. Working in back loops only, 1 sc in each sc to end of row. Turn. Rep last row until it measures 19 (20, 22, 24,)" Do not turn or fasten off . Work across long edge of Ribbing as follows:
  • Next row: (RS). Ch 1. Work 60 (63-65-73) sc evenly across. Turn.
  • Next row: Ch 3 (counts as dc). *1 sc in next sc. 1 dc in next sc. Rep from * to end of row. Turn.
  • Next row: Ch 1. 1 sc in rst dc. *1 dc in next sc. 1 sc in next dc. Rep from * to end of row, working last sc in top of ch 3. Turn. Next row: Ch 3 (counts as dc). *1 sc in next dc. 1 dc in next sc. Rep from * to end of row. Turn.
  • Rep Texture Pat for 19 (22,25,28) rows. Shape armholes: Next row: (RS). Sl st in each of rst 7 (7-9-9) sts. Ch 1. 1 sc in same sp as last sl st. *1 dc in next sc. 1 sc in next dc. Rep from * to last 6 (6-8-8) sts. Turn. Leave rem sts unworked.** cont pattern on rem sts until armhole measures 71⁄2 (8-81⁄2- 81⁄2)” Shape neck: Next row: (RS). Pat across 12 (14-14-18) sts (neck edge). Turn. Leave rem sts unworked.
  • Next row: Ch 2. Hdc2tog. Pat to end of row. Turn.
  • Next row: Pat to last 2 sts. Hdc2tog. Turn. 10 (12-12-16) sts.
  • Next row: Work even in pat. Shape shoulder: Next row: (RS). Sl st in each of first 5 (6-6-8) sts. Ch 2. Pat to end of row. Fasten off .
  • With RS facing, skip next 29 (29-31-31) sts. Join yarn with sl st to next st. Ch 3 (counts as dc). Pat to end of row. Turn.
  • Next row: Pat to last 2 sts. Hdc2tog. Turn.
  • Next row: Ch 2. Hdc2tog. Pat to end of row. Turn. 10 (12-12-16) sts.
  • Next row: Work even in pat. Shape shoulder: Next row: Pat across 5 (6-6-8) sts. Sl st in next st. Fasten off .
  • FRONT
    Work from ** to ** as given for Back. Cont in pat until armhole measures 6 (61⁄2-7-7)” ending on a WS row. Shape neck: Next row: (RS). Pat across 14 (16-16-20 sts (neck edge). Turn. Leave rem sts unworked.
  • Next row: Ch 2. Hdc2tog. Pat to end of row. Turn.
  • Next row: Pat to last 2 sts. Hdc2tog. Turn. Rep last 2 rows once more. 10 (12-12-16) sts. Cont even in pat until Front measures same length as Back to shoulder, ending on a WS row.
  • Shape shoulder: Next row: (RS). Sl st in each of rst 5 (6-6-8) sts. Ch 2. Pat to end of row. Fasten off . With RS facing, skip next 25 (25-27-27) sts. Join yarn with sl st to next st. Ch 3 (counts as dc). Pat to end of row. Turn.
  • Next row: Pat to last 2 sts. Hdc2tog. Turn. Next row: Ch 2. Hdc2tog. Pat to end of row. Turn. Rep last 2 rows once more. 10 (12-12-16) sts.
  • Next row: Work even in pat. Shape shoulder: Next row: (RS). Pat across 5 (6-6-8) sts. Sl st in next st. Fasten off .
  • FINISHING
    Sew shoulder seams. Sew in sleeves placing rows above markers along unworked edges of Front and Back to form square armholes. Sew side and sleeve seams. add a few rows of sc around neckline, work in a spiral until closed to desired tightness. Fasten off.
