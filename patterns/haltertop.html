Flower Halter Top

Yarn weight 3 (light)
hook size 4.5
I barely finished 1 skein of yarn for my size small.

BEGIN ON THE FLOWER MOTIFS

  • Slip knot, ch 7 Sl st into the 1st chain to connect Ch4, place 2 dc into ring, ch 1 Repeat place 2dc into ring, ch 1 Repeat until you have a total of 15 dc Sl st into the 3rd chain of the beginning ch3 Then Sl st into the 1st chain space
  • Round 2
    Ch 6, 1 dc into same space as previous round Ch 1, work into the next ch1 space below, place 1 dc, ch 3, place 1 dc in the same space, ch1 Working into the next ch1 space, 1 dc, ch 3, 1 dc in same space. Repeat until end of row. Ch1 and slip stitch into the 3rd chain of the beginning chain. Slip stitch through th 1s chain of the round
  • Round 3
    Ch 5, dc into same chain space ch2, 1 sc in the next chain space of 1 Ch 2, 1 dc in the next chain space of 3, ch 2, 1 dc in same space Ch 2, 1 sc in next chain space of 1 Ch 2, 1 dc in next chain space of 3, ch 2, 1 dc in same space Repeat until end of round Ch 2 and connect to 3rd chain of beginning chain with a sl st Sl st into 1st chain of the round
  • Round 4
    Ch 3 Place 2 dc into same space, ch 3 , place 3 dc into same space *Ch 2, place 1 sc into the sc of the previous round Ch 2, place 3 dc into the second space, ch 3, place 3 dc into the same chain space* Repeat ** until end of row Ch 2, connect to 3rd chain of beginning chain with a sl st Fasten off

TO CONNECT MOFITS

  • Begin on making 2nd flower by repeating round 1-4 of flower pattern, leaving only the last 2 petals of round 4 unworked
  • Place 3 dc in second to last petal space ch 1, connect to first flower motif petal with a slip stitch, ch 1 continue into the working flower motif with remaining 3dc Ch 2, place 1 sc into the sc of the previous round Ch 2, place 3dc in last petal space of the round Ch 1, connect to first flower motif petal with a slip stitch, ch 1 continue into working flower motif with remaining 3dc Fasten off
  • For size extra small, small and medium make and attach 1 more flower motif together (3 total) for large connect 2 more (4 total)

    • (for x-small, small and medium: when connecting the flower motifs, both outside flowers will have 2 petals along the top, the inside flower will have 1 petal along the top. For size large: both outside flowers will have 2 petals along the top, both inside flowers will have 1 petal along the top)

    TO BEGIN ON HALTER TOP

    • Extra small: attach your working yarn onto the far right (2nd petal) (pictured under the arrow)
    • Small: chain 5, then attach your working yarn to the far right petal
    • Medium: chain 10, then attach your working yarn to the far right petal
    • Large: attach your working yarn onto the far right petal
    • chain 7 (7,7,7) attach to next flower petal top loop with 1 sc
    • chain 15 (15,15,15) attach to next flower petal top loop with 1 sc
    • chain 15 (15,15,15) attach to next flower petal top loop with 1 sc
    • chain 7 (7,7, 15) attach to next flower petal top loop with 1 sc
    • For large only: chain 7 attach to next flower petal top loop with 1 sc
    • For small and medium only: chain (5,10) at the end of the row. .
      • .
    • Row 1
      chain 1, flip over work, continue down the row placing 1 sc in each chain.
    • Row 2
      chain 1, flip over work, continue down the row placing 1 sc in each chain. Repeat for a total of 20 (20, 24, 30) rows .
      • .
    • Next row
      Ch 1, flip over work, place 1 sc decrease in first chain of row, place 1 sc in each chain stop at the 2nd to last chain of the row, place 1 sc decrease in the last chain. Repeat last row 15 (15, 20, 25) times. .
      • .
    • TO FINISH OFF HALTER TOP .
      I added a SC edging along the sides and top of the halter top to finish off the sides, then added a picot stitch edging along the outside as well (for more detailed explanation refer to my youtube tutorial) Then add straps along the top and sides (each of my straps are 80 chains long. .
      • .
    • FILLING IN THE FLOWER MOTIF SPACES
      slip knot, ch 5, connect to 1st chain with a slip stitch ch 3, attach to flower motif with a slip stitch (each petal has 3dc, ch3, 3dc) (attach to middle chain of the first 3dc)
      • .
    .
    . .
    .

    Arrows show the points to connect, begin connecting to the point on the right and make your way around counter clockwise*

    • After you connect with a slip stitch, chain 3 and attach back onto your small ring with a slip stitch.
    • ch 3, repeat onto next point connecting with a slip stitch, ch 3 and attach back onto your small ring with a slip stitch. repeat all the way around, making a total of 4 connections.
    • To attach small ring, place hook though the center of the small ring and through the middle of space between the 2 flower motifs, attach with a slip stitch, Fasten off .

    repeat for all flower spaces.
